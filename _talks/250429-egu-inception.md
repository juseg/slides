### Global degree-day modelling

- CHELSA-W5E5 ca. 1 km input climate (T, P, σ)
- Temperature offset +4.0, +3.8, ..., -20.0 K
- Temperature-index accumulation and ablation model

~

→ Global **glacial inception threshold**<br>
